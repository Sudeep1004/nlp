formal dining area are designed for entertaining. The spacious media rm, with beautiful built-in custom cabinetry, offers stunning mountain views. The library offers views of

downtown as does the second bdrm and balcony. Each bdrm has an ensuite bath, & master bdrm also features an expansive walk-in closet and custom-built cabinetry for

extra storage. Two balconies offer almost 600 sq ft of space. Incredible location. Luxurious quality.
