Description

Welcome to this remarkable property located in Sheep River Valley of the Rural Foothills by Okotoks. Enjoy the panoramic views over the fresh spring creek as you sit in

the living room cozied up to the wood burning fireplace of this beautiful home. The charming 4 bedroom 5 bath, 4923 sq ft home also features kitchen with updated

appliances, living room, den, office and fully developed basement for all your family needs. Nature is at its best with a completely private yard featuring a lush array of

trees, plants & shrubs, patio, green house and 60' x 30' barn, with storage area is big enough for a tractor or horses. Beyond the creek is approximately 70 acres by the

Sheep River bank, being used as pasture.
