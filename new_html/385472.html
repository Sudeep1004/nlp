On 4.91 acres this 4135 sqft West Coast Contemporary ocean front estate offers privacy, breathtaking views and subdued luxury. A rare combination of attributes are

unmatched by any other residential offering on Vancouver Island. First growth repurposed Douglas fir timbers, yellow cedar, copper, regional stone, vaulted ceilings,

rooms filled by natural light from floor to ceiling windows. Architecturally designed with radiant heat throughout the home is anchored by the living rooms' 12 ft honed

Cambrian black granite fireplace book matched with the gourmet kitchen island. Entertain in the media room, on outdoor living spaces with hot tub and fire pit, dig clams or

shuck oysters on your 400 ft beach. Minutes to Comox Airport.
