Description

Court Ordered Sale. Bring all Offers! 12.87 acre site on peninsula with over 2,130 linear feet of waterfront. Adjacent to Moorecroft Regional Park. Shoreline consists of

mainly mild grade, rocky foreshore with 6 walk-on pebble beaches, allowing for easy access to the water through small inlets. Current land use controls allow for boat

moorage to floats with anchor blocks with an option for application and approval for a low profile boat ramp. There are 2 single family houses on the site, including a 2,000

+/- sqft 3 bedroom 1960's rancher and & 700 +/-sqft studio cottage. The site offers views of the Strait of Georgia and the coastal mountains, is forested with old growth fir,

arbutus, cedar, alder, maple & garry oak, and has extensive trail network. The RS-1 zoning allows for 1 hectare (2.47 acre) lots. All offers are subject to Court approval.
