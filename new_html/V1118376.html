Description

Stunning 65 acre private Gulf Island. Breakwater Island has it all! Sandstone beaches, eagles nests, nature paths, breathtaking views and the ultimate in privacy. Serviced

with hydro power and phone, the island also has lots of fresh water along with 2 homes, large workshop, good dock, boat ramp and much more. Watch eagles soar

overhead or watch the boats passing by the Gulf Islands or Silva Bay on Gabriola Island which has gas docks, a pub, retail stores and road access, next door. 4 titles gives

flexibility to sell portions of the island or keep entirely private.
