$5,495,000 - WE FOUND YOUR DREAM COTTAGE ON GEORGIAN BAY. You know how they say a picture is worth a 1,000 words - We'll let the photos do the talking.

Here are a few details of life on this private, 7 acre island: west facing, spectacular sunsets, deep water for boating, sheltered inlet, sand beach. Gorgeous open concept

cottage built in 2007; post and beam, fully insulated with additional 4' of foam insulation underneath, Pella windows and doors, connected to mainland hydro, hard wired

back up generator, kitchen with Viking and Sub Zero appliances, master bedroom with unparalleled views, 600 sq ft screened porch, pop up TV. Bonus: 3 bunkies, gym,

sauna.
