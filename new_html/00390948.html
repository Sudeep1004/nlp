Description

Whalesback..Dynamic contemporary living perched high on a solid granite cliff overlooking the Atlantic. Soaring ceilings, walls of glass, limestone heated floors, modern

Soapstone kitchen, elevator, luxurious infinity pool and a separate 2 level fully equipped guest house. Set on 2.7 acres with 450 of bold oceanfront bordering 1800 acres

of Crown Land. Library/office loft with panoramic ocean views, bedrooms all have ensuite baths. The master has a private terrace with outdoor fireplace and hot tub,

perfect for star gazing. A unique, amazing offering. This private setting is just 25 minutes to Halifax and 45 minutes to the airport.

Location Description

From Rotary drive to Purcells Cove Rd, left on Ketch Harbour Rd, left on Chebucto Head Rd, right on Duncans Cove Rd, Gannet Lane is to the right just before the cove.
