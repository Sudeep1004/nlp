Description

5.9 acres Nestled along the Pine Creek, the property is very private and just inside the City limits. You will not find a parcel like this with Creek edge, pasture for a couple

of horses and a very of the City across the valley. Privacy, privacy with towering spruce trees & hidden against a hill. a large home with many features such as a year

around solarium room off the kitchen, hobby room, two laundry rooms, 3 bedrooms on the main a huge living room with high ceiling & massive windows viewing the valley

and creek. Yes you have it all and in the City. On your own septic system and well it is independent of City services and so close to the centers and services. The outer

building is 60' long with a dance hall loft. Great storage for equipment or animals. A corral and fenced, previous owner had horses. the location is so outstanding and

private it is a little piece of heaven near the madding crowd, protected from noise by hill and trees. Quad garage has basket ball area with exceptional high ceilings.

wonderful potential for this land, possibly a church, small business. It is private, secluded and very picturesque with the creek winding along the edge of the land and the

beautiful treed valley. A private business location for a one person small quiet business. Located off a major highway, this is truly one of a kind property.
