hiking the mountain ridges, cross country skiing, mountain biking, horseback riding, skating, wildlife and landscape photography and nearby trout fishing in the Elbow

River. The white cedar log home features a chefs kitchen, family room with fireplace, spacious dining room, great room with floor to ceiling Waterton river rock fireplace, 3

bedrooms to sleep 10, 3 minutes to Bragg Creek for its many restaurants. No 2013 floods.
